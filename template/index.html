About Us

Welcome to StandHard

A Cloud-Based multiservice quality management System aiding Quality Control and Quality Assurance For Health Service providers, connecting patients with the right care, health information and records form any location via smart phone or device making medicine a reality through the power of data and artificial intelligence to solve a health problem and improve quality of lives across Nigeria and Africa at Large.

STANDAHARD is providing a quality management focused on providing confidence(ASSURANCE) that quality requirements will be fulfilled internally to management of STANDHARD and externally to consumers, health workers, government agencies, regulators, certifiers, and third parties.
